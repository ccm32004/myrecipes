Milk Bread

Ingredients

  • bread flour
  • water
  • whole milk
  • active dry yeast
  • salt
  • milk
  • sugar
  • butter
  • egg

Steps

  1. Make tangzhong.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in mixer.
  3. Add in wet ingredients and then the tangzhong.
  4. Proof the dough.
  5. knead the dough. Proof again.
  6. Cut off the dough into circular slices. Proof again.
  7. Apply eggwash. Bake the dough until it's bread.
